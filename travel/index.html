The Socialist Republic of Vietnam

Vietnam is one of the most beautiful countries in Asia. Spreading along the eastern edge of the Indochinese Peninsula, Vietnam covers a land area of 330.900 sq. km with the legendary world of Halong Bay, the colourful charm of Central Highlands and the criss-cross waterways of the Meking Delta. The country boasts a marvelous coastline of 3.260km dotted with white sandy beaches, tranquil bays and vivid coral reefs.
